How Social Recovery Works

Number of Shares

Select the number of shares that you wish to recover your secret from. This can be anywhere between 2 to 6. Make sure that this corresponds to the same number of shares that was initially selected when creating the shares

Entering a Share

Next, you'll be asked to enter the terms for each share. To do so, you can use the terms that are suggested or use our mnemonic secure keyboard. A share can consist of 24 terms. Make sure that you put the terms in the right order too
